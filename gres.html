Inspiration

Yaroslava Gres

The founder of Gres Todorchuk PR agency, Yaroslav Greshas been involved in cultural and social projects for five years.

These include the News Museum, Kyiv Sculpture Project, Euromaidan - History in the Making, Cirque du Soleil and Goethe-Insitut.

Before that, she was the youngest editor-in-chief of HELLO! in the world.

What are your secrets to maintaining speed?

You are the five people that surround you. I try to surround myself with people who are running so fast that I am out to catch them. Thanks to this I am showing good results, because I am equal to the strong ones. This is what drives me forward. If you are the only beacon, messiah and prophet, every word of which is law and light in darkness, is the path to nowhere.

I'm still trying to make the brain ache every day. To make it difficult for him to be faced with new tasks that are more difficult than previous ones. And of course, I'm learning. The same Cannes Festival of Creativity is a year-round trendsetter. I consider UAH 112,000 per ticket as the best investment in my speed.
